CLTCodes

A local place to learn programming

Concept:

CLTcode is a local group where programmers come together to collaborate. We have professionals from different industry backgrounds with more than 10 years of experience as well as people curious about the IT field. CLTcode Will focus on local projects to enhance the learning of new programmers. We share resources such as: local programming events, local skill enhancement projects, other online resources for new programmers.

Mission:

CLTcodes mission is to help you apply concepts you learn in programming courses in a real-life scenario. We will work through projects and help each other along the way.
